Sky Snacker

Serve fast. Catch the trash. Keep them happy.

The Story

Bridget always knew she wanted to fly. Not as a pilot — she wanted to be where the people were. As a little girl, she would line up her stuffed animals in rows of chairs, push a toy cart down the hallway, and serve them imaginary snacks with a smile so big it could light up a room. Her mom called it playing pretend. Bridget called it practice.

She got her wings at twenty-two. From her very first flight, she was hooked. The hum of the engines, the click of the overhead bins, the moment the cabin door closes and the whole world becomes her responsibility — she loved every second of it. While other attendants counted the hours until landing, Bridget counted the passengers she had made smile.

She is the one who remembers your name. The one who brings you a warm cookie before you even ask. The one who somehow knows you wanted a soda, not a juice, even though you mumbled it half-asleep. She does not just serve snacks — she makes people feel taken care of, thirty-five thousand feet above the ground.

And she loves to be busy. A full cabin is not a problem for Bridget — it is a challenge she was born for. The more requests coming in, the faster she moves, the wider she smiles. When the thought bubbles start stacking up and the trash starts flying back, that is when Bridget is at her best.

Today, Flight 2847 is packed to the last seat. Thirty passengers, three snack options, and one aisle. The fasten seatbelt sign just turned off. The requests are about to start rolling in.

Bridget straightens her hat, adjusts her scarf, and grips the cart. She was made for this.

How to Play

On desktop, use the up and down arrow keys to move Bridget along the aisle. Press 1, 2, or 3 to select a snack (Pretzel, Cookie, or Soda). Press the left or right arrow key to fling the selected snack to the passenger requesting it on that side.

On mobile, use the directional buttons below the game screen to move up and down. Tap a snack button to select it, then tap the left or right serve button to fling it.

The Passengers

When a passenger wants a snack, a thought bubble appears above their head showing what they want. The bubble starts white — they are patient. After a while it turns pink — they are getting annoyed. When it turns red and starts pulsing — you are almost out of time. If the bubble bursts, that is a miss.

If you fling the wrong snack to a passenger, that is also a penalty. Make sure you have the right snack selected before you serve.

The Trash

After a passenger has been served, they will eventually toss their trash back into the aisle. Bridget needs to be in that row to catch it. If the trash reaches the aisle and Bridget is not there, that counts as a miss too.

Scoring

Each correct delivery earns 100 points. Catching trash earns 25 points. Wrong snacks cost 25 points. Expired patience or missed trash costs 50 points. Five total misses and the game is over.

Serve enough passengers to advance to the next level, where everything speeds up.

Tips

Watch the bubble colors. Prioritize red bubbles — they are about to burst.

Do not forget the trash. It is easy to focus on serving and miss the trash sliding back toward you.

Stay near the middle rows so you can reach requests on either end quickly.

On desktop, keep your fingers near 1-2-3 so you can switch snacks fast.

The cabin is full. The cart is loaded. And Bridget would not have it any other way.
